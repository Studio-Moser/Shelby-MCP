# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Be kind and respectful.** Disagreements happen — handle them constructively. Focus on the idea, not the person.

**Be helpful.** If someone is stuck, offer guidance. We were all beginners once.

**Be patient.** Maintainers are volunteers with limited time. Issues and PRs will be reviewed, but not always immediately.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@studiomoser.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
